Recipe: Spaghetti Carbonara

Category: Main Course

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti (400g)
  • Pancetta or guanciale (150g)
  • Eggs (3 large)
  • Parmesan cheese (100g, grated)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Salt (for pasta water)

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions. Reserve some pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta or guanciale until crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese. Add black pepper to taste.
  4. Add the hot pasta to the pan with pancetta and mix well. Allow to cool slightly.
  5. Pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing quickly to create a creamy sauce. Use reserved pasta water as needed to adjust consistency.
  6. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan and black pepper on top.
