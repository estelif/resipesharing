Recipe: Caesar Salad

Category: Appetizer

Ingredients:

  • Romaine lettuce (1 large head, chopped)
  • Croutons (1 cup)
  • Parmesan cheese (1/2 cup, grated)
  • Anchovy fillets (4-5, optional)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Egg yolk (1, for dressing)
  • Lemon juice (2 tablespoons)
  • Olive oil (1/3 cup)
  • Dijon mustard (1 teaspoon)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Cooking Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg yolk, lemon juice, mustard, minced garlic, and anchovies.
  2. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking continuously to create a thick, creamy dressing.
  3. Season the dressing with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. In a large bowl, toss the chopped romaine lettuce with the croutons and grated parmesan cheese.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss until the ingredients are evenly coated.
  6. Serve immediately with extra parmesan on top if desired.
