Recipe: Chicken Pie

Category: Main Course

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (500g, cooked and shredded)
  • Onions (1 large, diced)
  • Carrots (2, diced)
  • Flour (2 tbsp, for thickening)
  • Chicken broth (2 cups)
  • Butter (50g)
  • Pie crust (ready-made or homemade)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Milk or cream (1/2 cup)

Cooking Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Melt butter in a pan and sauté onions and carrots until soft.
  2. Stir in flour to form a roux, then gradually add the chicken broth and milk/cream, stirring until the mixture thickens.
  3. Add shredded chicken, season with salt and pepper, and cook for a few more minutes.
  4. Pour the mixture into a pie dish and cover with the pie crust. Seal the edges and cut a few slits on the top to allow steam to escape.
  5. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown. Let it cool slightly before serving.
