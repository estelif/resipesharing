Recipe: Rice

Category: Appetizer

Ingredients:

  • Rice (2 cups)
  • Water (4 cups)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Butter (2 tbsp)

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice in cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Bring water to a boil in a large pot, then add salt and butter.
  3. Add the rice to the boiling water, reduce the heat to low, cover, and let simmer for 18-20 minutes or until all the water is absorbed.
  4. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.
