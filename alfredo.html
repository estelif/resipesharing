Recipe: Chicken Alfredo

Category: Main Course

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast (2 pieces)
  • Fettuccine pasta (200g)
  • Heavy cream (1 cup)
  • Parmesan cheese (1/2 cup)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Butter (2 tablespoons)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, and cook in a skillet with butter until golden brown. Set aside and slice into strips.
  3. In the same skillet, saut√© minced garlic until fragrant. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese and let it melt into the sauce.
  5. Add the cooked pasta and chicken strips to the sauce and toss to combine.
  6. Serve hot, topped with extra Parmesan if desired.
