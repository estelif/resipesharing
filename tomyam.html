Recipe: Tom Yam

Category: Main Course

Ingredients:

  • Shrimp (500g, peeled)
  • Lemongrass (2 stalks, crushed)
  • Galangal (sliced, 4 pieces)
  • Kaffir lime leaves (4)
  • Mushrooms (200g, sliced)
  • Chili peppers (2-3, to taste)
  • Fish sauce (3 tbsp)
  • Lime juice (2 tbsp)
  • Coconut milk (optional, 200ml)

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Bring water to a boil and add lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves. Let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Add mushrooms and chili peppers, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until they turn pink and opaque, about 3 minutes.
  4. Stir in fish sauce, lime juice, and coconut milk (if using), and simmer for another 2 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with extra lime wedges on the side.
