Recipe: Fettuccine Alfredo

Category: Main Course

Ingredients:

  • Fettuccine pasta (500g)
  • Butter (1/2 cup, unsalted)
  • Heavy cream (1 cup)
  • Parmesan cheese (1 cup, freshly grated)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the heavy cream and bring it to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low.
  4. Gradually add the Parmesan cheese while stirring constantly until the sauce becomes thick and creamy.
  5. Toss the cooked fettuccine in the sauce, ensuring all the pasta is coated evenly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
