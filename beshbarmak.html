Recipe: Beshbarmak

Category: Main Course

Ingredients:

  • Lamb or beef (1 kg, cut into chunks)
  • Onions (3 large, sliced)
  • Flour (2 cups, for dough)
  • Eggs (2)
  • Water (as needed)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Bay leaves (2)

Cooking Time: 2 hours

Difficulty: Medium

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Boil the meat with bay leaves, salt, and pepper for about 1.5 to 2 hours until tender. Remove the meat and slice it.
  2. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, eggs, water, and a pinch of salt. Roll it out and cut into wide strips or squares.
  3. Cook the noodles in the meat broth until done, then drain.
  4. In a separate pan, saut√© sliced onions in some of the meat broth until soft.
  5. Serve the noodles on a large platter, topped with the sliced meat and onions. Pour some broth over the dish.
  6. Serve hot with extra broth on the side.
